title: 'Inventory'
description: 'Manage furniture inventory records including stock levels and tracking'
---

The Inventory resource allows you to manage furniture inventory records, including tracking stock levels, monitoring low stock items, and updating quantities.

## The Inventory Object

<ResponseField name="id" type="integer">
  Unique identifier for the inventory record
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="productId" type="integer">
  ID of the associated product
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="quantityAvailable" type="integer">
  Current quantity of the product available in stock
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="minimumStockLevel" type="integer">
  Minimum stock level threshold for low stock alerts
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="lastRestockedDate" type="string">
  Timestamp of when the inventory was last restocked (ISO 8601 format)
</ResponseField>

## Available Endpoints

### CRUD Operations
- [List all inventory records](/api/inventory/list) - `GET /api/v1/inventory`
- [Get inventory record](/api/inventory/get) - `GET /api/v1/inventory/{id}`
- [Create inventory record](/api/inventory/create) - `POST /api/v1/inventory`
- [Update inventory record](/api/inventory/update) - `PUT /api/v1/inventory/{id}`
- [Delete inventory record](/api/inventory/delete) - `DELETE /api/v1/inventory/{id}`

### Stock Operations
- [Decrease stock](/api/inventory/decrease-stock) - `PUT /api/v1/inventory/decrease-stock`

### Filtering & Search
The [list endpoint](/api/inventory/list) supports multiple filters:
- `GET /api/v1/inventory/product/{productId}` - Get inventory by product
- `GET /api/v1/inventory/low-stock` - Get low stock items
- `GET /api/v1/inventory/out-of-stock` - Get out of stock items
- `GET /api/v1/inventory/available` - Get available stock
- `GET /api/v1/inventory/pagination` - List with pagination

## Common Use Cases

### Stock Monitoring
Track inventory levels and identify low stock or out-of-stock items using specialized endpoints.

### Inventory Management
Create and update inventory records to maintain accurate stock levels across your furniture catalog.

### Stock Updates
Decrease stock quantities when processing orders to maintain real-time inventory accuracy.
